Coverage Levels:

- Basic Coverage: Protects against common risks like fire, theft, and natural disasters (e.g., floods or storms).
- Advanced Coverage: Includes all basic coverages, plus additional protections like liability for accidents occurring on the property and minor repairs.
- Top Coverage: Comprehensive protection with high-value asset protection, extended liability, and coverage for accidental damage (e.g., accidental fires or unintentional damage).
